Symptoms & Cures

Headaches

Headaches can be caused by various factors including stress, dehydration, or tension. Common treatments include hydration, rest, and over-the-counter pain relief medications. For frequent or severe headaches, consult a healthcare professional.

Common Cold

The common cold is caused by viral infections and presents with symptoms like a runny nose, cough, and sore throat. Treatment focuses on relieving symptoms with rest, hydration, and medications such as decongestants or cough syrups.

Digestive Disorders

Digestive disorders such as acid reflux or irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) can be managed with dietary adjustments, medications, and stress management. It is important to consult a healthcare provider for a tailored treatment approach.
